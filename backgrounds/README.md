# Custom backgrounds

The easy way: in the app, click **Background → Add your photos…** and pick your images. The app downscales them to 2560px, fixes rotation, and stores them locally. Up to 20 photos. Landscape orientation looks best.

The folder way (for people running from source): drop images here named `ghibli-01.jpg` through `ghibli-20.jpg` (also .jpeg, .png, .webp) and the app picks them up on next launch. Photos added in-app take priority over this folder.
